# Yellow Talisay Fruit Training Images

Place images of **YELLOW (mature)** Talisay fruits in this folder.

## Image Guidelines:
- Clear, well-lit photos
- Fruit should be the main subject
- Various angles welcome (front, side, top)
- Minimum resolution: 224x224 pixels (recommended: 512x512+)
- Formats: JPG, PNG, JPEG

## Characteristics of Yellow Talisay:
- Yellow/golden skin color
- Mature/ripe stage (OPTIMAL for oil extraction!)
- Slightly soft flesh
- Highest oil content (~57-60%)

## Naming Convention (optional):
- `yellow_001.jpg`
- `yellow_002.jpg`
- etc.

## Note:
This is the MOST IMPORTANT category as it represents the optimal
harvest stage for maximum oil yield. Collect diverse samples!
