# Brown Talisay Fruit Training Images

Place images of **BROWN (fully ripe/overripe)** Talisay fruits in this folder.

## Image Guidelines:
- Clear, well-lit photos
- Fruit should be the main subject
- Various angles welcome (front, side, top)
- Minimum resolution: 224x224 pixels (recommended: 512x512+)
- Formats: JPG, PNG, JPEG

## Characteristics of Brown Talisay:
- Brown/dark colored skin
- Fully ripe to overripe stage
- Soft flesh
- Good oil content (~54-57%), slightly less than yellow

## Naming Convention (optional):
- `brown_001.jpg`
- `brown_002.jpg`
- etc.

## Note:
Brown fruits are still suitable for oil extraction but have
slightly lower yield than yellow (mature) fruits.
